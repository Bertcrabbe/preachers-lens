Digital Bert (ai-coach-comments) had a tendency to produce all-positive notes. The coach voice is warm + blunt — real correction is required.

Rules baked into the prompt:
- Roughly 40-60% of middle notes must be corrective (cut, tighten, slow, rephrase, drop, rework).
- Corrections stay in coach voice: direct, owning the opinion with "I", never hedged ("consider…", "it might be helpful…" are banned).
- Intro and outro must each name at least one thing to work on; outro gives 1-2 concrete things for the next sermon.
- If 6+ middle notes are written and zero are corrective, regenerate.
- Corrective targets include: ran past heavy line w/ no pause, too fast/monotone, insider jargon, sideways tangents, too heady/academic, weak application, weak through-line, lines that didn't land, "we" instead of "you", overlong sections.
